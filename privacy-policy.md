# Privacy Policy

**Cardnager**
Last updated: June 2025

## Overview

Cardnager is a offline desktop application. It does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your machine.

## What Cardnager does NOT do

- Does not collect any personal information
- Does not create user accounts
- Does not connect to the internet
- Does not send any data to any server
- Does not use analytics or tracking
- Does not use cookies
- Does not store any data outside of your own computer

## What Cardnager does do

- Reads and writes files on your local machine that you explicitly open
- Saves a local config file (window preferences, recent files list, session) to your system's app data folder
- Creates automatic backups in a `_backups` folder next to your files when you save

All of this data stays entirely on your device. Cardnager has no backend, no server, and no internet connection.

## Third Party Services

Cardnager does not integrate with any third party services.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes it will be updated in this repository with a new date.
